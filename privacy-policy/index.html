Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how ICBC Recovery Hub collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website.

Last updated: June 2026
Overview Information We Collect How We Use Information Partner Clinics Cookies & Analytics Security Medical Disclaimer Contact

Overview

ICBC Recovery Hub is an informational and referral website that helps visitors learn about recovery options after a motor vehicle accident and connect with affiliated clinics in Vancouver and Richmond.

By using this website or submitting information through our forms, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our website, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number or WhatsApp contact
  • Preferred contact method
  • General information about your recovery needs
  • Any additional information you choose to submit through our forms

We may also collect limited technical information automatically, including browser type, device information, pages visited, referral sources, and general website usage data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through this website to respond to enquiries, support referral requests, and improve the website experience.

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Help connect you with an appropriate partner clinic
  • Follow up regarding your request
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Understand website performance and visitor behaviour
  • Detect spam, misuse, or fraudulent activity

Partner Clinics

ICBC Recovery Hub does not directly provide medical treatment. If you request assistance through our website, your contact information may be shared with an affiliated clinic so they can contact you about your enquiry.

After your information is shared with a clinic, that clinic is responsible for handling your information according to its own privacy practices and applicable privacy laws.

We only share information that is reasonably necessary to help the clinic respond to your request.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors interact with the site and to improve the website experience.

We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity. These tools may collect information such as pages visited, device type, browser type, and general interaction data.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Information Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information submitted through our website.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, including partner clinics, external healthcare resources, or related services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of third-party websites.

Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking information about recovery services after a motor vehicle accident.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Canadian Privacy Protection

If you are located in British Columbia or elsewhere in Canada, your personal information is handled in accordance with applicable privacy legislation, including the Personal Information Protection Act where applicable.

Medical Information Disclaimer

The information provided on ICBC Recovery Hub is for general educational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding your specific condition.

ICBC Recovery Hub does not provide medical treatment and is not a healthcare provider. This website helps visitors access information and connect with affiliated clinics for assessment and care.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us through our website.
