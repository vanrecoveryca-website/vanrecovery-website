When to seek care

Signs your child may need support

More worries, fears, or clinginess
Avoiding car rides or familiar activities
Sleep changes, nightmares, or irritability
School, play, or family life affected

Why get support early?

Children may not always have the words to describe how they feel after a collision. Early support helps families understand changes, identify the right care, and build a recovery plan before daily life is affected.

People Also Ask

Children After a Car Accident FAQ

What changes might I notice in my child after a crash?+
Children may seem more worried, clingy, quiet, irritable, tired, or hesitant to ride in a car. They may also have sleep changes, nightmares, physical complaints, or changes at school and during play.
When should my child see a health professional?+
Arrange an assessment if changes are intense, persistent, worsening, or affecting sleep, school, friendships, play, or family life. Seek urgent care for concerning physical symptoms or immediate safety concerns.
Can family counselling help after a car accident?+
Yes. Family counselling can help children and parents understand emotional changes, develop coping strategies, and rebuild confidence in daily routines together.
What happens at a first counselling appointment?+
The clinician will ask about the collision, any changes you have noticed, your childâ€™s routines, and your familyâ€™s goals. Together you can make a plan that respects your childâ€™s comfort and pace.
Should parents attend counselling too?+
Often, yes. Parent involvement can help children feel secure and makes it easier to use helpful strategies at home, at school, and during travel.
When should I seek urgent help for my child?+
Seek urgent help for immediate safety concerns, serious physical symptoms, or a child who cannot be kept safe. Call 911 for an emergency or contact an appropriate local crisis or medical service.
Does ICBC cover counselling or psychology?+
Eligible customers may have up to 12 pre-approved counselling sessions and 12 psychology sessions within the first 12 weeks after a crash. Confirm your claim eligibility and any fee difference with ICBC or your provider.
How long does recovery take for children?+
Every child is different. Some changes settle within days or weeks, while others benefit from ongoing support. Early assessment can help identify the right next step.
