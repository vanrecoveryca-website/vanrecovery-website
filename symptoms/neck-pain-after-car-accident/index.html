Neck pain after a car accident

Neck pain, stiffness, and whiplash symptoms are common after motor vehicle accidents. Learn what symptoms to watch for and explore possible ICBC recovery support options in Vancouver and Richmond.

Start ICBC recovery assessment View treatment options

Common symptoms

Neck stiffness, reduced range of motion, shoulder tension, headaches, and whiplash-related discomfort.

Possible causes

Sudden force from a collision may strain muscles, joints, ligaments, and soft tissues around the neck.

Recovery support

Physiotherapy, active rehab, massage therapy, acupuncture, and other care options may be considered after assessment.
