    What is Covered?

    100% Coverage with direct billing for eligible claims. No referral needed. Most patients pay $0.

    Pre-authorization

    ICBC typically pre-authorizes 12 sessions for Counselling and Physiotherapy. This means you can start immediately.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about ICBC claims and car accident recovery.

    Most patients do not need to pay upfront for ICBC-approved treatment.

    • If your claim is active and the treatment is eligible, clinics can often bill ICBC directly.
    • Many patients have little or no out-of-pocket cost at the time of treatment.
    • Coverage depends on your claim status and approved services.

    Yes. ICBC commonly covers physiotherapy after a car accident in British Columbia.

    • Neck and back pain
    • Whiplash injuries
    • Muscle stiffness
    • Reduced mobility

    Many ICBC patients begin physiotherapy shortly after opening a claim, and treatment is often direct billed to ICBC.

    ICBC may cover counselling if emotional or psychological symptoms appear after a collision.

    • Anxiety or stress
    • Fear of driving
    • Sleep disruption
    • Emotional distress

    Not all physiotherapy or rehab clinics provide counselling services in the same location, so some patients look for more integrated recovery support.

    Many ICBC patients experience both physical injuries and emotional stress after a car accident.

    While some clinics focus mainly on physical symptoms like pain or mobility issues, some patients may also need support for anxiety, stress, fear of driving, or emotional recovery.

    This is why some patients prefer a more integrated recovery approach that considers both physical and emotional recovery together.

    ICBC patients in Vancouver may look for physiotherapy, rehabilitation, massage therapy, counselling support, or integrated recovery care after a car accident.

    Aetheris Integrated Health in Vancouver supports ICBC-related recovery with a multidisciplinary approach focused on both physical and emotional recovery.

    Pegasus Integrated Health in Richmond provides ICBC physiotherapy and rehabilitation support for patients recovering after motor vehicle accidents.

    ICBC physiotherapy may help with neck pain, back pain, whiplash, muscle stiffness, and mobility issues after a collision.

    A multidisciplinary car accident recovery clinic combines multiple recovery services under one integrated care approach.

    • Physiotherapy
    • Kinesiology
    • Massage therapy
    • Active rehabilitation
    • Counselling support

    The right clinic depends on your recovery needs, not just location.

    • Whether you need physical treatment, emotional support, or both
    • Experience working with ICBC patients
    • Integrated versus separate services
    • Convenience in Vancouver or Richmond
