    ICBC Treatment & Coverage

    We coordinate your physical and mental care through the ICBC Enhanced Care framework.

    What is Covered?

    100% Coverage with direct billing for eligible claims. No referral needed. Most patients pay $0.

    Pre-authorization

    ICBC typically pre-authorizes 12 sessions for Counselling and Physiotherapy. This means you can start immediately.

    Coverage Options

    Insurance & Direct Billing

    We support ICBC direct billing and work with many extended health insurance providers. Coverage depends on your provider, policy, treatment type, and claim eligibility.

    ICBC Direct Billing

    Eligible ICBC claims may qualify for direct billing with little or no upfront payment.

    Extended Health

    Some physiotherapy, massage therapy, counselling, and rehab services may also be covered through extended health plans.
